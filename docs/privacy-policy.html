Privacy Policy for PageNotes

Last Updated: 11 FEB 2025

1. Introduction

PageNotes ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the PageNotes Chrome extension ("the Extension").

2. Information We Collect

PageNotes does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension operates entirely on your local device and stores data within your Chrome browser.

a. Notes Data

  • Notes you create using PageNotes are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`.
  • Your notes are never sent to external servers or third parties.
  • Notes are associated with the hostname of the website where they were created.

b. Permissions Requested

To provide its core functionality, PageNotes requests the following permissions:

  • `storage`: To save and retrieve your notes locally.
  • `activeTab`: To determine the current website hostname and associate notes accordingly.
  • `scripting`: To manage encryption and decryption of your stored notes securely.
  • `host permissions`: To identify the domain where notes should be stored and retrieved.

PageNotes does not track your browsing activity or modify web pages.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since PageNotes does not collect personal data, we do not process or share any information. All notes remain private and accessible only on your device.

4. Data Security

  • PageNotes encrypts your notes before storage using a locally generated encryption key to enhance security.
  • Since all data remains on your device, unauthorized access is prevented unless your device is compromised.

5. Third-Party Services

PageNotes does not use third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs. Your data is never shared with any external party.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
